Privacy Policy — Progress Fitness Tracker

Last updated: March 26, 2026

 

1. Who we are

Progress is a personal fitness tracking app developed by Pim Croon. If you have questions about this policy, contact: pimcroondb@gmail.com

 

2. What data we collect

When you use Progress, we collect and store the following data that you provide directly:

 

Account & profile data

•       Email address and display name used to create your account

•       Optional profile picture (stored locally on your device only, never uploaded to our servers)

 

Body & fitness data

•       Body metrics: weight entries, body fat percentage, and body measurements (neck, shoulders, chest, arms, waist, hips, thighs, calves)

•       Fitness activity: workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight, set type, duration, distance), cardio activities (type, duration, distance, calories)

•       Workout templates: template names and the exercises they contain, stored so you can reuse them across sessions

•       Notes: free-text notes you attach to workouts or individual exercises. These are stored on our servers as part of your workout data. Avoid including sensitive personal information in notes.

•       Nutrition: food entries including food name, meal type, and macronutrients (calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat)

 

Body profile & goals

•       Age, biological sex, and height — used to calculate your estimated daily calorie needs (TDEE) using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula

•       Activity level — used as a multiplier in calorie calculations

•       Goal weight, goal type (cut / bulk / maintain), weekly rate target, and starting reference weight

•       Maximum heart rate or age-based heart rate — used to calculate your personal heart rate training zones

•       Weekly workout goal

 

Note: biological sex and age are health-related data points. Under GDPR, these are processed with extra care and are collected solely for the purpose of calculating personalised fitness and nutrition estimates. They are never used for profiling or shared with third parties.

 

App feedback

•       Messages you submit via the in-app feedback form

 

Apple HealthKit data

We request access to Apple Health (HealthKit) to read and/or write the following:

 

Read:

•       Body weight and body fat percentage

•       Heart rate, resting heart rate, and heart rate variability (HRV)

•       Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2)

•       Respiratory rate

•       Sleep analysis (total, deep, REM, core, and awake duration)

•       Step count

•       Active energy burned — used to adjust your daily calorie budget in the Nutrition module

•       Workouts and cardio activities — used to display exercise history in the Cardio module

 

Write:

•       Body weight — when you log a weight entry in the app, it is optionally saved back to Apple Health

 

HealthKit data is used solely to display your health trends within the app and, if you choose, to provide context to the AI Coach feature (see section 4). It is never shared with third parties for advertising, data mining, or any purpose unrelated to the health and fitness features you have explicitly enabled. This complies with Apple’s HealthKit guidelines.

 

3. How we store your data

Your account and fitness data — including workout logs, templates, notes, nutrition entries, body metrics, and profile data — is stored securely on Supabase (supabase.com), a hosted database platform, on servers located in the European Union (EU West — Ireland). Data is protected with row-level security, meaning you can only access your own data.

 

Your profile picture and AI Coach conversation history are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to our servers.

 

Data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data is permanently and immediately deleted from our servers.

 

4. AI Coach

Progress includes an optional AI Coach feature powered by Claude, a large language model developed by Anthropic (anthropic.com). This section explains exactly what data is used, how, and what is not shared.

 

What is sent when you use the AI Coach

When you send a message to the AI Coach, the following is transmitted to our Supabase Edge Function and processed by Anthropic’s API:

•       Your message text

•       Your conversation history (up to the last 100 messages)

•       Anonymised health context — limited only to the data sources you have enabled in the AI Coach screen

 

The health context you can optionally enable includes:

•       Current weight and goal weight (Weight source)

•       Recovery score, sleep hours, HRV, resting heart rate, and step count (Recovery source)

•       This week’s cardio activity summaries — type, duration, distance (Cardio source)

•       Your weekly workout goal

 

What is NOT sent

The following data is never sent to the AI Coach, even if it exists in the app:

•       Your full workout history, workout templates, or exercise notes

•       Nutrition logs

•       Body measurements

•       Age, biological sex, height, or activity level

•       Any data from a source you have not explicitly enabled

 

Storage & retention

AI Coach conversations are stored only on your device in local app storage. They are not stored on our servers and are not retained by Anthropic beyond what is needed to generate a response. Conversations are cleared when you sign out or use the Clear Conversation option.

 

Usage limits

The AI Coach is limited to 10 messages per day to prevent abuse and protect the service.

 

Anthropic’s role

Anthropic processes data transmitted through their API in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy. By using the AI Coach feature, you explicitly consent to this processing. You can disable the AI Coach at any time in Settings.

 

5. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

As a Dutch developer, this app is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / AVG). We process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

•       Consent: you explicitly consent to data collection when creating an account, when granting HealthKit access, and when enabling the AI Coach feature

•       Contract performance: processing is necessary to provide the app’s core functionality

•       Legitimate interest: processing of age, biological sex, and height to calculate personalised fitness estimates, where this processing is proportionate and expected by users of a fitness app

 

You may withdraw your consent at any time by disabling integrations in Settings or by deleting your account.

 

6. What we do not do

•       We do not sell your data to anyone

•       We do not share your data with advertisers

•       We do not use your data for data mining or third-party analytics

•       We do not use your data for any purpose other than running the app for you

•       We do not collect data in the background when the app is closed

•       We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, data mining, or third-party analytics

•       We do not store AI Coach conversations on our servers

•       We do not use workout notes or free-text fields for any purpose other than displaying them back to you

 

7. Third-party services

The app optionally integrates with the following services:

 

Strava

If you connect your Strava account, we fetch your activity data from Strava’s API. This requires you to explicitly authorize the connection. You can disconnect at any time in Settings.

 

Open Food Facts

When you scan a barcode in the Nutrition module, a request is sent to the Open Food Facts public API to look up food data. No personal data is sent in this request.

 

Anthropic (Claude AI)

If you use the AI Coach feature, your messages and the health context you have selected are processed by Anthropic’s Claude API. See section 4 for full details. Anthropic’s privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy.

 

Firebase Crashlytics (Google)

We use Firebase Crashlytics (Google) to collect crash reports and diagnose technical issues. Crashlytics automatically collects the following when the app crashes:

•       Device model and iOS version

•       App version and a random installation UUID

•       Crash stack traces

This data is used solely to fix bugs and improve app stability. It is never linked to your identity or shared for advertising purposes. For more information, see Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

 

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR/AVG, you have the following rights:

•       Right to access: request a copy of all data we hold about you by emailing pimcroondb@gmail.com

•       Right to deletion: delete your account at any time from the Profile screen — this permanently deletes all your data

•       Right to portability: request your data in a portable format by emailing pimcroondb@gmail.com

•       Right to rectification: request correction of inaccurate personal data by emailing pimcroondb@gmail.com

•       Right to object: disconnect Strava, revoke HealthKit access, or disable the AI Coach at any time in Settings

•       Right to lodge a complaint: you may file a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl

 

9. Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us at pimcroondb@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

 

10. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this document and notify users via the app where required. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
